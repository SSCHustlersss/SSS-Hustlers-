📝 General Rules

  • Passage upar box mein dikhega — dhyan se padho aur type karo.
  • Neeche wale box mein type karo — copy-paste allowed nahi hai.
  • Backspace use kar sakte ho lekin time waste hoga.
  • Timer shuru hone ke baad band nahi hoga.
  • Time khatam hone pe automatically submit ho jaayega.
  • Section A complete karne ke baad Section B aayega.
  • Net Speed = Gross Speed − (Errors × Penalty)
  • KDPH = Total key depressions × (3600 / seconds taken)

💡 Scoring

  • Gross Speed: Total words typed ÷ minutes taken
  • Net Speed: Gross Speed − error penalty
  • Accuracy: (Correct chars ÷ Total typed chars) × 100
  • KDPH: Total keystrokes per hour
⚠️ Dobara attempt mat karna — ek hi baar milega. Timer shuru hone ke baad band nahi hoga.
